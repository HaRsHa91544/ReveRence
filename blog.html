Female rugby players join the Laborex Senegal teams!

On 12 September 2023, Laborex Senegal and the CFAO Solidarité foundation signed an agreement with the Senegalese Rugby Federation. The agreement will enable players in the women’s national team to train as delivery drivers with the Training Centre for Port and Logistics Professions (CFMPL).
